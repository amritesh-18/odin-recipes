Samosa

Description:

Samosa is a popular Indian snack consisting of a crispy, triangular pastry filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat. Typically deep-fried to golden perfection, it offers a delightful crunch and a flavorful, aromatic filling. Often enjoyed with chutney, samosas are a beloved street food and party appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • all-purpose flour
  • carom seeds (ajwain)
  • vegetable oil
  • boiled potatoes
  • green peas
  • green chillies
  • some spices
  • salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix: Combine 2 cups flour, 1/2 tsp carom seeds, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  2. Knead: Add water gradually to form a firm dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat Oil & Cook: Add 3-4 mashed potatoes, 1/2 cup peas, and optional 1 chopped onion.Stir in 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp red chili powder, and salt to taste.
  4. Shape: Divide dough into balls, roll into ovals, and cut in half.
  5. Fill: Form cones, fill with potato mixture, and seal edges with water.
  6. Fry: Fry samosas until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
Enjoy with mint or tamarind chutney :)
